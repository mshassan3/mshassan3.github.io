Welcome to my website!

Hi! My name is Mehnaz Hassan and my friends call me May. I attended UC Merced where I received my Bachelor's of Science in Applied Mathematics. Later, I attended Fresno Pacific University where I received my preliminary single subject teaching credential. After two years, I obtained clearance of my single subject teaching credential for mathematics with Merced City School District. Now, I am excited to be pursuing my Master's in Learning Technologies and Design degree. I started my journey as a mentee during my fieldwork experience at UC Merced, then continued my path as an educator teaching sixth grade math for a year in Dos Palos, CA. During the last four years, I taught seventh grade math. This academic school year, I am excited to be teaching eighth grade industrial arts (technology). I see opportunity for growth as I am teaching eighth graders and a new curriculum (Paxton Patterson).
