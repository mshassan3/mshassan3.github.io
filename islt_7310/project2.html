Project 2

Nearpod

In the last few years of teaching, I have learned making the lesson interactive and engaging makes it more enjoyable for the teacher as well as the students. I have learned about quite a few different programs in the process of searching for a platform that was user friendly as a teacher and engaging for students. I found that learning and using Nearpod has been a great resource. Nearpod developers are continuously updating their website with more features that can be added on to teacher presentations to make learning more engaging and interactive for students.

When I was first learning how to use Nearpod, my professional learning community and I created the "Dueling Discounts" Nearpod lesson. Then, I challenged myself with creating one with as many different possible activites from nearpod as I could with my "First Days of School" Nearpod lesson. Recently, I have been using Nearpods that are already created and modifying them to meet the students' needs.
